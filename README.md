# 🛒 Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
The Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard is a Power BI project focused on analyzing grocery sales data to identify business trends, customer preferences, outlet performance, and product category insights. The project combines data visualization and exploratory data analysis techniques to generate meaningful business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance across Blinkit outlets
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Compare sales across outlet sizes and locations
- Understand customer preferences using fat content and rating analysis
- Visualize business trends using interactive dashboards

# 📈 Key Business Insights

## 🔹 Sales Overview
- Total sales generated across all outlets reached approximately ₹1.20 Million.
- Average sales per item were around ₹141.
- Total number of items sold was 8523.

---

## 🔹 Outlet Performance
- Tier 3 outlets generated the highest revenue among all outlet locations.
- Medium-sized outlets contributed the highest share of overall sales.

---

## 🔹 Product Insights
- Fruits & Vegetables, Snack Foods, and Household items were top-selling categories.
- Seafood and Breakfast categories generated comparatively lower sales.

---

## 🔹 Customer Preference Insights
- Low-fat products recorded higher sales compared to regular-fat products.
- Average customer rating remained around 3.9, indicating positive customer satisfaction.

---

## 🔹 Trend Analysis
- Sales performance increased gradually and peaked around 2018.
- After 2018, sales remained relatively stable.

---

# 📉 Exploratory Data Analysis
The project also includes Python-based exploratory data analysis using:
- Scatter Plots
- Bar Charts
- Distribution Plots
- Violin Plots
- Correlation Heatmaps

The correlation analysis revealed weak linear relationships among numerical variables, suggesting that sales are influenced by multiple combined business factors.
